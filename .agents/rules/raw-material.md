# Raw Material Rules

- Raw material is source evidence, not polished writing.
- Preserve original wording and filenames when possible.
- Do not clean up raw notes in place.
- If curation is useful, create or update a page in `wiki/`.
- After review, move the original source from `raw/unprocessed/` to `raw/processed/`.
- Link curated pages back to processed raw sources.
