# Wiki Page Rules

- Use Obsidian-friendly Markdown.
- Use wikilinks for internal references, such as `[[Related Page]]`.
- Add YAML frontmatter with `aliases`, `tags`, `status`, and `sources` for curated knowledge pages.
- Operational pages such as `wiki/index.md`, `wiki/log.md`, and category `README.md` files may use simpler maintenance formats.
- Keep curated pages concise and useful.
- Prefer synthesis over copied raw excerpts.
- Add a `Related` section when cross-links are useful.
- Update `wiki/index.md` when a page is created, renamed, merged, or retired.
