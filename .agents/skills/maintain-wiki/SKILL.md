# Maintain Wiki

Use this skill for cleanup, structure checks, duplicate detection, stale pages,
broken links, and category review.

## Workflow

1. Inspect `schema/` to understand the current operating model.
2. Review `wiki/index.md` for known hubs and categories.
3. Look for duplicate or near-duplicate page titles.
4. Check whether important pages are missing from the index.
5. Search for stale, empty, or source-less pages.
6. Look for raw notes that deserve curation.
7. Check for broken or ambiguous wikilinks where practical.
8. Propose small schema changes before large reorganizations.

## Bias

- Prefer merging and linking over creating more folders.
- Prefer small schema changes over sweeping taxonomy redesign.
- Preserve raw sources even when curated pages improve.
